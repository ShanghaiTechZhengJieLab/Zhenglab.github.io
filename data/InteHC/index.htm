Supplements of the manuscript:

Identifying Protein Complexes from Heterogeneous Biological Data

Min Wu, Zhipeng Xie, Xiaoli Li, Chee-Keong Kwoh and Jie Zheng

Introduction

We proposed an approach called InteHC (Integrative Hierarchical Clustering) to identifying protein

complexes from Heterogeneous Sources. The following figure shows the framework of InteHC.

 

First, we use individual sources/features to compute the score matrices that store various protein affinities.

Second, we construct a final score matrix, which is the weighted sum of the matrices from individual features.

In particular, these weights are learned by a supervised model (i.e., a linear ranking SVM) on a known set of

positive and negative protein-protein interactions.

Third, a hierarchical clustering algorithm is then applied to identify protein complexes from the final score matrix.
